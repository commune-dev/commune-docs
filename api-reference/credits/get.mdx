## Response

<ResponseField name="data" type="object">
  Your current credit balance.

  <Expandable title="properties" defaultOpen>
    <ResponseField name="included" type="number">
      Credits from your monthly plan. Resets to your plan's included amount on each billing cycle reset. Does not roll over.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="purchased" type="number">
      Credits from purchased add-on bundles. Rolls over month-to-month and does not expire.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="total" type="number">
      Total available credits (`included + purchased`). This is the number checked before each SMS send or phone number purchase.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="usedThisCycle" type="number">
      Total credits consumed since the last billing cycle reset. Useful for analytics and budgeting.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="cycleResetAt" type="string">
      ISO 8601 timestamp of the next billing cycle reset. On this date, `included` credits are restored to your plan's monthly allocation.
    </ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

<Note>
**Monthly included credits by plan:**
- Free: 200 credits/month
- Agent Pro: 500 credits/month
- Business: 5,000 credits/month
- Enterprise: Unlimited

Included credits reset monthly. Purchased credits roll over indefinitely.
</Note>
