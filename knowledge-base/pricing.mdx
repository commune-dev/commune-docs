---
title: How much does Commune cost?
description: Commune has four plans: Free ($0), Agent Pro ($20/mo), Business ($150/mo), and Enterprise (custom). Plans differ by email volume, inboxes, and features.
---

## The short answer

Free to start. $20/mo for agents running in production. $150/mo for teams running multiple agents at scale.

No credit card required to create an account. You can send and receive email on the free plan immediately.

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Emails/day | Inboxes | Key features |
|------|-------|------------|---------|--------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 100 | 1 | Basic send/receive, shared domain |
| **Agent Pro** | $20/mo | 10,000 | 10 | Custom domain, warmup, structured extraction, webhooks |
| **Business** | $150/mo | 100,000 | Unlimited | All features + network graph, semantic search, SSO, audit logs, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Custom | Custom contracts, SLA, on-prem options |

## What's included on every plan

Regardless of plan, every Commune account includes:

- **Ed25519 agent authentication** — challenge-response identity for agents, not shared secrets
- **DKIM / SPF / DMARC** — full email authentication stack configured automatically
- **Spam protection** — inbound spam filtering and sender reputation scoring
- **Prompt injection detection** — inbound emails scanned for embedded prompt injection attempts
- **REST API + SDKs** — Node.js, Python, and MCP integrations

## How to upgrade

Go to [commune.email/dashboard/billing](https://commune.email/dashboard/billing) to upgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

For Enterprise — custom volume, SLA, or on-prem requirements — contact sales directly. Reach out at [commune.email/dashboard/billing](https://commune.email/dashboard/billing) and select "Contact sales."

No credit card is required to start on the free plan.
